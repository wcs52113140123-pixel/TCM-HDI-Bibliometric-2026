## §4.0 Discussion lead-in (~130 words, framing only)

A methodological motif throughout this work is **multi-method triangulation**. 
Where a finding is independently supported by bibliometric convention analysis 
(the bibliometrix R package, applied to the WoS subset; §3.1), citation burst 
detection (CiteSpace, applied to the same subset; §3.2), structural 
cross-analysis (the topic x mechanism Fisher enrichment introduced here; 
§3.4-§3.5), and herbal-taxonomy stratification (the three-tier herb x mechanism 
analysis introduced here; §3.6), we treat that finding as robust to any single 
method's assumptions. Signals appearing in only one method's view are flagged 
for cautious interpretation rather than discarded. The strongest convergent 
example -- *Hypericum perforatum* (St. John's wort) as a CYP3A4 inducer, 
recovered independently by all four methods (bibliometrix authorship-network 
Q2 frontier; CiteSpace Era 1 burst; Topic x Mechanism Fisher enrichment in 
topic #5; three-tier herbal FULL_CHAIN exemplar) -- anchors the case-based 
discussion that follows.
